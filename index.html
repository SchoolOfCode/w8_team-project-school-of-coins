School of Coins: Blackjack

Bet Amount: 0 credits

The Rules:

  1. The goal of BlackJack is to beat the dealers hand without going over 21
  2. Face cards(Royals) are worth 10 & Aces are worth 1 or 11
  3. Each player starts with two cards, and one of the dealer's cards is hidden until the end of the round
  4. To 'HIT' is to ask for another card
  5. To 'STAND' is to hold the value that you have and end your turn
  6. If you go over 21 you are then bust, the dealer will win regardless
  7. If you are dealt 21 from the start (Ace & 10), you get BlackJack and win

Good Luck and have fun!
